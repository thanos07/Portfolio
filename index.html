PROJECT 04 — CLINICAL AI SIMULATION · MAY 2026

Medikit

AI Clinical Training Simulator

STATUS: DEPLOYED
ReactViteFastAPIAnthropic ClaudeGroq API

Problem

OSCE-style clinical training traditionally needs actors, supervising clinicians, and physical rooms — logistics-heavy and hard to scale for repeated trainee practice.

Build

A browser-based simulator covering 18 OSCE-style cases across cardiology, surgery, respiratory, GI, endocrine, neuro, OB/GYN, paediatrics, renal, and psychiatry. Each case runs a Claude-driven patient persona for natural conversation, then an attending-grader model scores the trainee against case-specific criteria and published guidelines (ESC, NICE, BTS, RCOG). A Groq fallback keeps the simulator available if the primary Anthropic API is unreachable.

Outcome

Deployed end-to-end (Vercel frontend, Railway backend) as a free, always-on simulated OSCE environment that gives trainees structured, guideline-grounded feedback without real-patient risk.
